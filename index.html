How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

Also, all of the tiles are invisible! New tiles flash light, and combined tiles flash dark.

Modified by Edwin Zhang, originally created by Gabriele Cirulli. Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and conceptually similar to Threes by Asher Vollmer.
